Baraj Valea Drăganului

This photo captures the Baraj Valea Drăganului (Valea Drăganului Dam) in Romania, taken on a warm July day in 2024. Surrounded by the lush greenery of the Apuseni Mountains, the dam, built in the 1970s, creates Lake Drăgan, a key reservoir for hydroelectric power. Rising 120 meters (393 feet) and spanning 424 meters (1,390 feet), the dam blends seamlessly with the natural landscape, while the calm lake, fed by the Drăgan River, provides a peaceful contrast to its engineered structure. The scene reflects the harmony between the dam and the vibrant forests that frame it.
